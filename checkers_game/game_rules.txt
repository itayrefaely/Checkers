Game Rules for American Checkers

1. The game is only played on the dark squares.
2. Pawns can only move forward diagonally.
3. When a pawn reaches the opposite side of the board
   it is promoted to a king.
4. Kings can also move backwards but only one square at
   a time (** no jumping ! **).
5. Capturing is done by jumping over an opponent's piece and
   landing in the empty square on the other side.
6. You can capture multiple pieces in a single turn as long
   as you make a continuous diagonal line of captured pieces.
7. If a capture is possible in the current move then you
   ** must capture ! **.
8. If you have multiple possible captures you can choose
   which one to make.
